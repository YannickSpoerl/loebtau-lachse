But now for real...

Okay. You think this is some kind of Joke. Yes and No. We are no Frisbee-Club, we don't play in any League and have no members; at least not officially. But we are real! We really live in a shared apartment next to the Bonhoefferplatz and we play frisbee there several times a week! We have many friends and playmates that join us playing frisbee on a regular basis... and YOU are welcome as well! Just loudly call "Löbtau-Lachse" into the Bonhoefferpark and keep your eyes out for flying frisbees and you will most certainly find us!
